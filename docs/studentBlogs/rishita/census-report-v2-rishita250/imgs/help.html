Using the Interactive Map

Here's how to make the most of your experience with the interactive digital access map:

Zoom In and Out for Detailed Viewing

See both national and local patterns by zooming in or out.

Hover for State Data

Hover your cursor over any state border to view the overall percentage of households without internet for that state.

Hover for Counties

Click into individual counties for hyper-local digital access data.

Toggle State or County Views

Use map controls to show or hide state and county layers depending on your viewing preference.
